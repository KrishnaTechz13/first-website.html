Frequently Asked Questions

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No, all processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

How accurate is the background removal?

Our tool uses advanced AI algorithms to achieve near-perfect background removal. For best results, use images with clear contrast between subject and background.

What image formats are supported?

We support all common image formats including JPG, PNG, WebP, and BMP. The result will be downloaded as a PNG with transparency.

Is there a file size limit?

There's no hard limit, but very large files (over 20MB) may cause performance issues on some devices.
