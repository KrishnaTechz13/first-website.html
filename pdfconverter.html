Frequently Asked Questions

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No, all processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

What are the maximum image dimensions?

You can process images up to 8000×8000 pixels, but larger images may take longer to process.

Is there a file size limit?

There's no hard limit, but very large files (over 20MB) may cause performance issues on some devices.

How many images can I add to a PDF?

You can add as many images as you want, but very large PDFs might take longer to generate.
